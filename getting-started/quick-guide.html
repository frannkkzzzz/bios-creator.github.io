Quick Start Guide

BiosCreator is designed for ease-of-use in mind. Even without user guide, you should be able to figure out how it works.

General Procedure

Double-Click the BiosCreator App in Start Menu.

Now, depending on your intent, select the corresponding buttons:

  • Repair or clean ME/TXE Region
  • Repair BIOS Region
  • Split EC Firmware and System BIOS
